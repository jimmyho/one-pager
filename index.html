1993 - First Pascal program compiled, awesome!
1994 - Started a BBS, and officially became a Fidonet node.
1995 - Learned ANSI codes for own art projects.
1995 - VB for DOS with clickable buttons!  Boom!
1995 - Wow'ed school design class with my VB/Win animation and Ace of Base background music...ouch.
1996 - Launched PalStation, an online social site with best of HTML2.0!  Had no clue about SQL database at the time, in fact had no clue about anything else but 'pretty' HTML.

1997 - The invitable happened.  Enrolled in Computer Engineering at University of NSW, Sydney.
